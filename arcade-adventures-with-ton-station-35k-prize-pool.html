Arcade Adventures with TON Station: $35k Prize Pool

January 20, 2025
Announcements
By
Tom
  • Facebook
  • Twitter

Explore Arcade Adventures with TON Station and ChainGPT

Take a step into a world where classic arcade gaming meets the modern blockchain. TON Station and ChainGPT invite you to join ‘Arcade Adventures with TON Station’. 

As pioneers of Web3 gaming, TON Station has set the benchmark for Web3-based Telegram games with titles like Rocky Rabbit, Spin Town, Catizen and more!

We’re plugging in a second controller for this campaign… let’s dive into what it means for both our communities and how you can win from the $35,000 prize pool!

Your Mission 

We’ve teamed up with TON Station to make this campaign special for all our gamers on-chain.

It’s simple: bring the TON Station mascot to life in a retro arcade setting. 

Picture the mascot battling space invaders, racing through neon tracks, or uncovering treasures in cosmic dungeons. Turn your ideas into a custom NFT and showcase your creativity.

How to Participate

  1. Create Your NFT
    Use the TON Station app to design your custom TON mascot NFT.
  2. Mint Your NFT
    Turn your design into a digital collectible.
  3. Share Your Work
    Post your NFT on X/Twitter using the campaign text and tags. Let others see your creation.

The Fun Doesn’t Stop There!

Make sure to complete these additional tasks and earn more entries:

  • Follow @tonstationgames and @ChainGPT on X/Twitter.
  • Join the TON Station and ChainGPT Telegram communities.
  • Subscribe to TON Station’s YouTube channel and CoinMarketCap profile
  • Start using  the TON Station Telegram app

This Won’t Be Here Forever…

Make sure to mark your calendars! 

  • Start Date: January 20, 2025
  • End Date: February 11, 2025

We will be posting regular updates over on Twitter/X and also Telegram, so be sure to make yourself a member of our community in preparation of our campaign.

What is TON Station?

TON Station is a premium game distribution platform built by industry leaders SIDUS HEROES and SuperVerse. This platform goes beyond the ordinary, offering exclusive games and content you won’t find anywhere else in the Web3 space. 

By collaborating with top Web3 gaming studios and global partners like Crypto Banter, Alex Becker, and EllioTrades, TON Station connects users with unmatched gaming experiences and expertise.

The platform is thriving, with over 19 million app users and 2.9 million daily active users. 

Why Participate?

This campaign is your chance to bring your imagination to life in a digital collectible. Showcase the possibilities of Web3 and the unique features of TON Station’s ecosystem. Your creation could inspire others.

Create, mint, and share your custom NFT today and be apart of the $35,000 prize pool.

Start your arcade adventure and show our communities your creativity!
